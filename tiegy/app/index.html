3 SIMPLE STEPS TO CLEARER, YOUNGER LOOKING SKIN

STEP 1: Cleanse And Refresh To Lay The Foundation For Clearer, Smoother Skin

Your skin is under attack every day.
Dust, sweat, smoke, pollutants in the air and those dangerous cancer causing molecules known as free radicals are all wreaking havoc on your skin.
This is why cleaning your skin is the first step towards a better complexion.
Many face washes available tend to use harsh ingredients that strip your skin of its natural oils. Once these oils are gone, your skin cells are thrown out of balance leading to them lose their moisture.
The Tiege Hanley Daily Face Wash gently cleans dirt, grime and excess oil without drying out your skin.
We also included eucalyptus and lavender to help relax and calm your skin, reducing redness and inflammation.
The daily wash both washes away the rigors of the day and leaves your skin feeling refreshed and moisturized.

STEP 2: Exfoliate To Unclog Pores, Remove Dead Cells And Regenerate New Skin

The next step is to use an exfoliating scrub to get rid of the old, dead skin cells on the surface of your skin. This promotes the growth of new skin cells leading to clearer, younger looking skin. Exfoliating also helps reduce fine lines by removing the damaged surface cells from your face. Tiege Hanley’s Scrub gently removes these dead skin cells without damaging your skin using crushed apricot seeds. B3 vitamins, cucumber extract and menthol add a refreshing, cool and soothing effect on your skin. Then we added one of the most effective skin care ingredients around: allantoin. Discovered over 100 years ago, allantoin promotes and accelerates skin regeneration and growth. So not only are you getting rid of dead cells, but you’re actually encouraging new ones to grow at the same time.

STEP 3: Moisturize, Protect And Rejuvenate Your Skin For A Glow Everyone Will Notice

Our skin is the first thing that interacts with the outside world. It’s also the first layer of protection from dangers in the environment. Every day when you step outside your skin is bombarded by pollution, smoke, dust, harsh elements like wind and snow and deadly UV-A and UV-B rays from the sun. These all lead to what’s known as transepidermal water loss or in other words… your skin loses water and loses it quickly. The result is dry, leathery skin that makes you look years older than you really are. With the Tiege Hanley AM Moisturizer we’ve packed it with anti-inflammatory, anti-microbial and even anti-oxidant ingredients like lavender, calendula, rosemary and plantain extract. And the SPF20 sunscreen protects you from the sun’s rays without leaving you feeling oily.

WHY DOES YOUR WASH PRODUCT NOT FOAM A LOT?

The facial cleanser is designed to be gentle. Foaming only means lots of surfactants and does not equate to cleaning the skin. When cleansers have high foaming, the chemicals that facilitate this action also deplete skin lipids that lead to dehydrating the skin. Our WASH product contains very low concentrations of surfactants, so it cleans while helping to maintain skin lipid balance making it possible to moisturize while cleaning.
